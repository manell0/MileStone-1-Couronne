Story

Couronne, or coron, also called corona is a variant of the board game that is believed to have originated in Asia or Africa

The game is played The game is played by two to four players who are divided into two teams. Each team must try to hit their fifteen rings into the holes on the game board. Queues and special bumps are used for this. The team that gets their rings down first wins.

In the 1930s the Swedish Couronne Championship was held at Nordiska Kompaniet's department store in Stockholm.

Couronne has taken some ideas from Carrom which is a similar board game Carrom (also spelled carom/carroms) is a tabletop game of Indian origin.
