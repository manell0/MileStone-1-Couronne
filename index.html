The goal of the game As in billiards, it's about getting all your own balls down, but in this game it's about your own rings. Preparations and layout Lay out the red and yellow rings in the circle in the center of the game board. Story

Couronne, or coron, also called corona [1] is a variant of the board game that is believed to have originated in Asia or Africa. [2] The game is played by two to four players who are divided into two teams. Each team must try to hit their fifteen rings into the holes on the game board. Queues and special bumps are used for this. The team that gets their rings down first wins.
